Editor Tips

  • Click directly inside any text cells of Date, Description, Debits, Credits, or Statement Balance to edit.
  • Standardize date strings to YYYY-MM-DD. Numbers are auto-parsed when clicking "Re-Calculate".
  • Delete incorrect, phantom, or metadata rows by clicking the trash icon at the end of each row.
